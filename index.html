Question 1: Article on Illegal Mining

Illegal mining has been a persistent problem in our area, leading to numerous negative effects. Firstly, it causes severe environmental degradation. The landscape is scarred with open pits, and the water bodies are contaminated with harmful chemicals used in mining. This not only destroys the natural beauty but also affects agriculture and local livelihoods. Secondly, illegal mining operations are often unsafe, resulting in frequent accidents and loss of lives.

To control illegal mining, stricter enforcement of laws is necessary. The government should increase patrols and monitoring of mining areas. Additionally, raising awareness about the long-term consequences of illegal mining among the local communities can help discourage this practice.

    1. untimely: premature
    2. expedition: adventure
    3. grievous: severe
    4. perishing: dying
    5. sprinted: ran quickly

The writer watched as Sapato swam, but when Sapato struggled, the writer, unable to swim well, could not save him. Realizing there was no one around to help, the writer ran home in distress. The incident led the writer to change his behaviour.
